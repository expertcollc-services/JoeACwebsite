Read This First: Back Office Instructions

This screen controls your whole business website and marketing. Do these in order every time.

  1. Open Settings. Add phone, email, address, and tracking IDs. Click Save Settings.
  2. Open Integrations. Connect Google, Bing, Yahoo, BBB, and directories you use.
  3. Open Website Content. Change homepage text and images. Save when done.
  4. Open Services, Locations, and Promotions to update what customers see.
  5. Open Shop / Blog to post products and articles with images or media.
  6. Open Leads every day. Move new leads to Contacted, then Booked, then Closed.
  7. Open Traffic and Directories weekly to check what channels are bringing real jobs.

Simple rule: Type information, click the save button in that section, then check the table below to confirm your update appears.

    Integration Hub

    Ad and directory integration-ready system

    Connect and track platforms like Google, Bing, Yahoo, BBB, Yelp, and more from one place.

    How to use Integrations

    1. Use Type filter to show Ads or Directory platforms.
    2. Search by name (example: Google, Bing, BBB, Yelp).
    3. Click Connect on each platform you use.
    4. Use Open to verify listing or ad account pages quickly.
    5. Click Connect Core Platforms to auto-connect your main channels.

    Company Profile and Tracking

    Keep phone, address, and tracking IDs updated so analytics and campaigns remain accurate.

    How to use Settings

    1. Fill in business name, phone, email, hours, and address exactly as customers should see it.

    3. Turn on lead notifications and add recipient emails.
